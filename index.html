Important caveats
  • Single-center retrospective derivation; external validation pending.
  • The score complements clinical judgment — it does not replace it.
  • Designed for pre-procedural risk estimation, not for triage of acute SDH.
  • Operating-point cutoffs (≥5 for Model 1, ≥4 for Model 2) were chosen for clinical interpretability, not to maximize Youden's J.
